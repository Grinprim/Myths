Culture

Born of stars and dreams, the Eladri believe themselves descended from the echoes of forgotten constellations. They dwell in crystalline cities suspended between dimensions, where light refracts into living color. Their scholars study not only arcane forces, but the geometry of existence itself. Outsiders see them as distant, but their curiosity for mortal emotion often draws them into the wider world.
