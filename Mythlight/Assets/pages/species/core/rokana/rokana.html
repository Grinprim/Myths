Rokana

The Under-Folk

Overview

Most people could go their whole lives without meeting Rokana, they are a rather secluded folk. They live either beneath the soil, or beneath the sea, often in secluded settlements, away from most other peoples.

They cherish simplicity and down to earth life, often finding pleasure in the land around them, either as farmers, gardeners, miners etc. They are not as technologically advanced as other races, though not that they couldn’t be, they just are taking it slow.

Appearance

While Rokana are rather diverse with their two subspecies, they do share a few common features. Firstly, they are very small, being only ~0.6 units (~3ft) tall. While their height is a very noticeable feature, their tails and antennae are not far off.
Their arthropodal features extend across their whole body, parts of their body are covered with a chitinous exo-skeleton. Of their two subspecies, one is aquatic, having features of prawns, and another is subterranean, having features of centipedes.
Their appearance branches off quite heavily based on their Subspecies.

Pelagic Rokana — Denizens of the Deep

Pelagic Rokana have smooth, curved forms shaped by the sea. Their chitinous growths are centered near their tails and antennae, as the rest of their body is covered in a smooth skin, akin to a dolphin.
They range in multiple different colors, from salmon pink to toad green, often depending on where they live.
Their bodies are adorned with stripes, dots or markings, as a form of camouflage, to protect themselves from the dangers of the deep sea. While their hair comes in virtually any color. Their eyes are humanoid, however have a secondary skin they can cover them with to swim in the salty sea. They are adept swimmers due to their very flexible bodies, that can contort to most any shape, so they can fit into very tight spaces.

Chthonic Rokana — Scholars of the Underground

Chthonic Rokana are covered in far more chitin than their Pelagic brethren. Their bodies are more durable to better withstand impacts and scrapes from living and digging underground. With non chitinous areas still having a rather rough skin.
Their skin is colored like different stones or minerals, usually grays to browns, however some do come in more exotic colors, like that of gemstones. Their hair can come in a variety of colors, though most common are whites and grays. Their bodies are slender and thin, so they can fit into tight cracks easier.
Their eyes emit a faint glow, and can come in virtually any color. They do not rely on their eyes a lot while underground, so some Rokana are known for having poor vision, nothing glasses couldn't fix.
They are very adept at using their tails to climb and support themselves, having it as almost a secondary appendage. While their antennae are often fluttering about as a way to feel and touch around, useful in tight tunnels.

Culture

Pelagic Rokana — Denizens of the Deep

Pelagic Rokana have lived near the seas for ages, so when other species moved nearby, they were quite quickly trading and working together. They were expert fishers and thus could offer in exchange for sturdier building materials and better housing their skills. Nowadays Rokana usually run port cities, due to their knowledge of the seas and expertise.
They believe in the seas, specifically they call to the aspect of festivity and fortune, to grant them safe passage among the currents.
In day to day life, they tend to underwater gardens, or keep pets. Often they travel due to sheer intrigue. While traveling it is common to carry a flask with water from your home, so you are never far from it.

Chthonic Rokana — Scholars of the Underground

Chthonic culture is very rigid in a way. They are far more reserved, and enjoy their own solitude, over mixing with others. That is not to say that they don't, but the deeper you go underground, the less you see mixing.
They are very religious folk, following the great light. Each city has a central lantern in the middle, which shines during days, and dims at night, upkeep and cleaning are duties cherished by many. As such the lights have also developed a sort of protection, Chthonic Rokana train to protect their encampments from underground threats, fighting instead of running away.
The day to day life of these Rokana involves craftsmanship, from carving to sculpting to metalwork, they enjoy using the resources around them to make something new. While they do this, they always focus on not taking more than they need, as to not upset the balance of the world.
