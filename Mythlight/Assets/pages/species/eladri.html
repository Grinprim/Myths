Eladri

Children of the Stars

Overview

The Eladri are an ancient people who have lived longer than any other race. Upon reaching maturity, they cease aging entirely, granting them near-immortal lives.

They dedicate themselves to knowledge, seeking to understand both the mortal world and the vast cosmos, whether grounded on the material plane or dwelling within Astral Cities.

Appearance

They are medium in stature, slightly shorter than an average human, and possess a presence that feels faintly luminous. All Eladri share several defining traits, despite cultural division. Their skin is always vibrant, hues of twilight blues, deep violets, molten golds, rose quartz pinks, nebula greens, or silvered lavender.
Their ears are elongated and slope slightly downward. While their eyes are enlarged and fully saturated in color, iris and sclera blending into a single field, their eyes are often compared to gemstones due to this.
In addition, every Eladri bears a tail, long and expressive, usually tapering with a soft tuft of hair at the tip. Their tails are often used in communication, as they react expressively to the emotions that the Eladri exhibit.
Their hair textures vary wildly, from straight silken hair to tight spirals and curls. White hair is common among many lineages, but so too are deep midnight blacks, blues, and rare metallic hues as well.

Culture

Eladri culture revolves around knowledge and scholarship, they are often researchers, scholars, mages or other professions that seek knowledge. Raised to study and record the cosmos from a young age, they form a bond with the stars, treating them as an extension of their own family.
They will often consult the cosmos on daily tasks, or invite it to dine with them symbolically, by resting a plate of food on the window sill. Rumors say that sometimes the food does go missing, though no one is quite sure where it goes.

Eladri live virtually forever, thus certain things like birthdays become rather obsolete, their perception of time is rather skewed, they will often not notice long stretches of time as such. Years feel like hours, months like minutes, to an Eladri, a long wait could mean the downfall of an entire civilization.
Due to this immortality, they often do not enter romantic relationships with mortals, not that they could make children with them anyway, as Eladri physiology is incompatible with most other races.

Subspecies

The Eladri split long ago into two cultural paths: the Wild Eladri and the Astral Eladri.

Appearance

Wild Eladri are instantly recognizable by their horns; branching, deer-like antlers that grow naturally from their skulls. These horns are semi-crystalline living structures, faintly translucent at the tips. Within them shimmer star-like motes that glow softly in darkness, resembling the night sky.
No two horn sets are identical. Some curve elegantly backward like crescent moons; others spread wide and intricate like the branches of fate. Small charms, chains, and carved runes are often woven into them. Breaking a horn is a profound trauma, both physically and spiritually.

Culture

Wild Eladri live alongside mortals, as they separated themselves from their Astral brethren long ago. They embraced the mortal world, finding their comfort in the advent of mortal knowledge. Due to this, they often find themselves as scholars, mages and librarians, tending to vast archives for years, though always leaving time to explore more of the world. Traditions have become less important to them, as they have learned from the mortals around them to cherish life as it comes, seeing pleasure in the moment instead of following rigid traditions from times long gone. Though some of this behaviour still peeks through, they still are connected to the Cosmos afterall.
They will often form tight knit communities in settlements, finding comfort in others like themselves, creating almost a mini Astral City. The atmosphere of these communities is often rather ethereal. The sky is filled with stars, regardless of time of day, the smell of Eladran food permeates the air. It is a true reflection of their heritage.

Appearance

Astral Eladri lack horns. Instead, their defining feature is a dusting of star freckles scattered across their cheeks, temples, shoulders, and sometimes down their tails. These freckles shimmer faintly like constellations in the night sky and subtly brighten when they channel magic. They often wear clothing that highlights their markings, while still being quaint and composed.
No two sets of markings are the same, they are a reflection of the personality, wild and erratic, or calm and composed. They are usually silver, or white in color, although rarely they can come in almost any color.

Culture

Astral culture is far more rigid, organized and structured. They are a reflection of their traditions, studying the cosmos, cataloging their discoveries and working tirelessly to store as much knowledge as possible.
Downtime involves meditation, tea, peace and reading. However there are the more rowdy, that still wish to be expressive in their nature. Art, Sports, Showmanship and Writing are all present in the cities. While rigid, the culture is still free, you are your own individual, while working for a greater purpose.
Astral cities are a reflection of ancient Eladri culture, high peaks and towers surrounded by an eternal night sky. The Eladri who dwell there reflect the same endurance. They live without the erosion of age, dedicating centuries to study, refinement, and contribution. What others might call monotony, they experience as life.

History

✦ The Great Creation
Eladri were created at the moment of the great Creation, they are one of the oldest species. They are also the last remaining Immortal species, as the others have long died out or lost their immortality.
✦ The Age of Radiance
All Eladri lived in peace on the Mortal plane, alongside developing nations. Living atop mountains in their citadels. They watched both the cosmos and the world around them.
✦ The Fall of the North Star
A great cataclysm causes a divide between the Astral and Wild Eladri. Wild Eladri are exiled to the Mortal world, while the Astral Eladri isolate their Citadels in a pocket dimension, Wild Eladri learn to live with the mortals.
✦ The Silent Era
While tensions settle there is virtually no communication between the two sects of Eladri. Mortals slowly forget about the existence of the Astral Cities as a whole.
✦ The Great War
During the Great War, Eladri are drafted into the military, some are given shelter in the Astral cities and hide away.
✦ Present Era
Wild and Astral Eladri are no longer at odds, however Astral cities are still off limits to mortals and Wild Eladri, baring a few exceptions.
